## Owners can now transfer device ownership to another user.
With this release, device owners can transfer ownership of their devices directly in the Tedee app. This feature is designed for situations like selling, gifting, or changing responsibility, and streamlines the handover process.

To make the transfer, the owner simply enters the email address of the new user. The app will also display a list of devices paired with the one being transferred and suggest them for inclusion. The owner can decide which paired devices will be moved along with the main device; devices that are not selected will be automatically unpaired and will remain on the original account.

After completing the transfer, all previous shared access to the device is removed, and the new owner receives exclusive control and management rights. This ensures both device security and a clean handover, without the need for support assistance.
