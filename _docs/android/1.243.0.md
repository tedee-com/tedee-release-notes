## More reliable Auto-Unlock on Xiaomi devices

Auto-Unlock now works reliably on Xiaomi phones, even with the screen off and the phone locked. Previously it could fail unless the screen was on, because Bluetooth stopped detecting nearby locks. Your lock is now detected consistently as you approach.
