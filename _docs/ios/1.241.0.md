## Faster app launch after cold start—improvements make opening the app faster after it’s been killed and relaunched
We’ve improved the startup time after a “cold launch” (when the app is fully closed or killed and then reopened). Most users will notice the Tedee app opens quicker and is ready to use sooner in these situations. Please note: after updating to this version, your very first launch may still be slower than usual, but future cold starts will benefit from these speed enhancements.

## Improved error handling when adding Tedee GO to Apple Home—failed commissioning now stops properly so users know when to try again
This update addresses an issue that occurred specifically with Tedee GO locks when adding them to Apple Home (via Matter). If a problem occurs during the commissioning process, the app now halts the process and clearly informs the user, making it easier to know when and how to retry. This prevents confusion and ensures you won’t get stuck on unclear errors during setup.

## Updated PIN code lists for lock owners when using keypad over Bridge—pull to refresh now reliably shows the latest PINs
If your Tedee keypad is connected via Bridge (not Bluetooth), you may have seen outdated PIN lists in some cases. Now, simply using pull-to-refresh on the PINs list will always fetch the latest codes directly from your device, giving owners the most accurate, up-to-date view of active PINs.
